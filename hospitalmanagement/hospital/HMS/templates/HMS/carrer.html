Dubai Hospital: The development of Dubai is visualized since the mid 1970s. DOHMS have taken decision to construct a specialized hospital because of increase in population and diseases in 1977. The Dubai Hospital started its operations from March 1983. It helped a lot in improving medical services in Dubai. Hospital have very good infrastructure. It contains 14 stories in which lower two for emergency and rest of the part is for wards. Dubai hospital is known for latest and best medical treatment of its patient. It is also working in the area of medical research.
